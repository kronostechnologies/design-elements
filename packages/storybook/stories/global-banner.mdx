## Definition
A global banner is located under the top header of the application, displaying a general message for the product or system, which has an impact on the entire application [1].
<Canvas of={GlobalBannerStories.Neutral} />

## Usage
### When to use
- Use to communicate important information affecting the entire application or system [1].
- Use to display messages initiated by the application or system, independently of user action [2].

### When not to use
- Do not use to display notifications concerning specific pages [1].
- Do not use in response to user action during a specific task. [2].

## Variants
### Neutral
Neutral global banners are used to deliver supplementary information on a global level. This variant is used to inform users of an element that may assist or affect their experience without grabbing full attention.
- Can be dismissed by the user or the application, depending on the content.
- Does not necessarily require an action.
<Canvas of={GlobalBannerStories.Neutral} />

### Warning
Warning global banners are used to display information that needs the user's attention or that requires the user's action to avoid potentially dangerous actions or errors.
- Can be dismissed by the user by clicking on the "ignore" button.
- Does not necessarily require an action.
<Canvas of={GlobalBannerStories.Warning} />

### Alert
Alert global banners are used to communicate specific problems or situations that the application might be going through, that may affect the user's journey or require a specific action from the user.
- Can't be dismissed by the user.
- Dismisses automatically once the error is resolved.
- Can block the user from proceeding until the issue has been resolved.
<Canvas of={GlobalBannerStories.Alert} />

### Discovery
Discovery global banners are used to inform the user about something new affecting the whole application that may enhance their experience.
- Can be dismissed by the user or the application, depending on the content.
- Does not require immediate action.
<Canvas of={GlobalBannerStories.Discovery} />
