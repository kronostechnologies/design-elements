## Definition
Pagination allows users to browse through content displayed on multiple pages.
<Canvas of={PaginationStories.Default} />


## Usage
### When to use
- Pagination is commonly used for things like table listings, search results, and directories.
- Divide large quantities of data or content into chunks.
- Make user comprehension of data/content less overwhelming.
- Enable all users to navigate through pages or locate a specific page number.
- Show how many pages of content there are and how many results have been returned.

### When not to use
- Do not use pagination for a transactional service or a form, use the progress tracker instead.
- Do not use when your DB is too heavy to upload everything upfront.
